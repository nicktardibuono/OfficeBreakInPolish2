OfficeBreakInPolish

  • High Concept: Break into the office and steal the questions for your upcoming interview.
  • Player Experience Goals: Have fun, avoiding enemies and collecting questions.
  • Genre: Platformer/Arcade
  • Game play: The player moves left and right and jumps using the arrow keys.
  • Prototype goal: Have a camera follow and scale images to cover the whole screen.
  • Features: Allows players to control the ememies. Enemes travels between two given x values.
  • Setting/Premise: The office for your upcoming interview.
  • Story: It is Sunday afternoon and tomorrow mourning you have an interview for the biggest job opportunity in your life, but you are worried that you might screw it up. Instead of relaxing and getting a good night of sleep you decided to break into the heavy guarded facility and steal the questions to better prepare yourself. Get the questions and don't get caught. Good Luck.
  • Target Audience: Casual Gamers
  • Play time: Can take as long as you want, but normally only a minute or two.
  • Strategies: Timing jumps to avoid enemys.
